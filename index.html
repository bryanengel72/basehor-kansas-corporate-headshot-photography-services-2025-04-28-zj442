Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my corporate headshot?

We recommend professional attire that aligns with your industry standards. Solid colors work best, particularly navy, gray, or deep blue. Avoid busy patterns or bright colors that might distract from your face. We'll provide a detailed styling guide upon booking.

How long does a typical session take?

Individual sessions usually take 30-45 minutes, including time for outfit changes and different poses. Team sessions are scheduled based on group size, with approximately 15-20 minutes per person.

What's included in the final delivery?

You'll receive a comprehensive package including high-resolution files for print, web-optimized versions for digital use, and specifically cropped images for LinkedIn, business cards, and other social media platforms. All photos are professionally retouched.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

Your professionally edited photos will be delivered within 5-7 business days via a secure digital gallery. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee, with 24-48 hour turnaround time.

Do you offer on-location services?

Yes, we provide on-location services for team photos and corporate events throughout the Basehor area. We bring our professional lighting and backdrop setup to your office for maximum convenience.
